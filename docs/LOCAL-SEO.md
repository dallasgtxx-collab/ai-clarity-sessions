## Production maintenance

1. Verify the domain in Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.
2. Submit `https://aiclaritysessions.com/sitemap.xml`.
3. Inspect the homepage and Dallas page with Google Rich Results Test and Schema.org Validator.
4. Keep class dates accurate and mark past sessions completed.
5. Add approved reviews only through the moderation workflow; do not add aggregate rating schema until enough genuine, eligible reviews exist and the markup follows search-engine policy.
6. If a verified staffed business address and phone become publicly available, evaluate a Google Business Profile and expanded local schema at that time. Do not substitute a virtual or fabricated address.
7. Publish genuinely useful local content only when it answers a distinct learner or organization need; avoid cloned suburb pages.
